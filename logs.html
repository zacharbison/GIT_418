There are lots of things that help scripts on the internet learn about what/who is accessing them. While browsers are constantly updating their security features it is still a good idea to arm yourself with the knowledge of how to stay safe on-line. Bellow are a few links that can help you get started.


https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/how-stay-safe-web

Here Firefox web browser lists steps to keep it's users safe. Many of the practices apply to other browser users as well.


https://www.us-cert.gov/publications/securing-your-web-browser

This US government website explains the hows and whys of securing your web browser. Most Major browsers listed with links to their support pages.


https://www.irs.gov/newsroom/seven-tips-to-protect-your-computer-online

The IRS gives 7 tips to protect your information


https://sos.fbi.gov/

The FBI's Safe Online Surfing website teaches kids about internet safety. Grades 3 though 8.
